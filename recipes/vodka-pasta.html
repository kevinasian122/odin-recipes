Penne alla vodka

A creamy tomato and vodka based pasta sauce perfect for any meal

This recipe uses aromatics combined with tomato paste and vodka, and a little bit of spice, to create a perfect blend of creamy deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • 450g of pasta (penne or rigatoni preferably)
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/3 cup of vodka
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 shallots, minced
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • lots of Parmesan cheese
  • 4.5-oz tube of tomato paste
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. melt the butter in a pan over medium heat and saute garlic and shalots until soft, about 5 mins
  2. add red pepper flakes and tomato paste, saute another 3-4 mins
  3. add vodka to deglaze, scrape up the bottom of the pan until alcohol has boiled off
  4. add heavy cream and salt and pepper, stir to combine for creamy sauce, let it come to boil then turn off the heat
  5. add parmesan cheese, as much as you can grate or to taste, and stir to combine
  6. add 450g/1lb of pasta to boiling salted water and cook according to package instructions
  7. combine everything, serve with more parmesan and optionaly chopped basil, and enjoy!
